THE CONCEPT

        

        After taking the detailed brief we will present the concept designs, 
        consisting of floor plans and elevations illustrating the proposed furniture 
        layouts and any structural changes that may be required. We also present 
        mood board presentations containing samples of all the proposed fabrics 
        and finishes as well as imagery of suggested furniture and joinery concepts.
        

    
    
THE FINAL

    

        Having approved all of the concept designs we progress to the design 
        development and detail design stage. All final bespoke furniture, joinery 
        and interior architecture drawings will be presented, along with all final 
        finishes artwork and accessories for clients to approve. At this stage we will 
        also present a fully itemised breakdown of costs.
    

   
         
THE BRIEF

         

            We build a picture of the project through a site visit or floor plans and 
            photographs as well as ensuring that we understand all factors such as time 
            scale, budget, and any structural requirements.
